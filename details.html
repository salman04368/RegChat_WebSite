About Us

We are a team of University of Bahrain students who experienced the frustration of navigating complex university regulations. Searching through hundreds of pages was time‑consuming and confusing.

That's why we created RagChat – an intelligent assistant that makes university information accessible 24/7, in both Arabic and English. Our mission is to empower students with instant, accurate answers.

2 Languages Supported
24/7 Always Available

Features

Designed to help you navigate university regulations with speed and clarity

Bilingual Support

Ask questions naturally in English or Arabic. RagChat automatically detects your language and responds in the same language with a casual, student‑friendly tone. No switching or translation needed.

Auto language detection Conversational responses

Smart Memory

RagChat remembers your last 5 turns of conversation, so you can ask follow‑up questions without re‑explaining context. Understands “yes”, “no”, and option numbers seamlessly.

5-turn memory Contextual follow‑ups

Accurate Answers

Hybrid search (keyword + semantic) extracts precise information including specific percentages, deadlines, and requirements. No more vague references – get exact numbers like “75% attendance required”.

Exact percentages Detailed rules

Precise Intent Recognition

When your question is ambiguous, RagChat asks a single, smart clarifying question instead of guessing. This reduces confusion and ensures you get the exact information you need – whether about in‑person attendance, e‑learning, or exam rules.

Ambiguity detection One‑question resolution

Voice Input

Powered by Whisper AI, voice input achieves over 95% accuracy in both Arabic and English. Just speak your question – RagChat transcribes it and provides an instant answer.

95%+ accuracy Arabic & English

Lightning Fast

Thanks to Groq's ultra‑fast GPU inference, you receive text answers in 1‑3 seconds and voice responses in 3‑5 seconds. No waiting, no lag.

1‑3 sec (text) GPU optimized

Comprehensive Coverage

All 622 pages of official University of Bahrain regulations are indexed and searchable – from attendance and exams to grading, registration, and academic policies.

Full regulation index Up‑to‑date

Privacy First

Your conversations are session‑based and temporary. No personal data is stored, tracked, or shared. Close the app – your chat history disappears.

No data storage Session only
